# SkyMetrics Platform Overview

SkyMetrics is an enterprise-grade aviation analytics platform designed to support
next-generation airlines with integrated operational, commercial, and customer
experience intelligence.

The platform is built to align with modern airline operating models, enabling
data-driven decision-making across Flight Operations, Engineering, Network
Planning, Commercial Strategy, and Customer Experience.

complex aviation data into actionable intelligence.

SkyMetrics is designed with:

## Key Functional Domains

SkyMetrics supports analytics across the following domains:

### 1. Flight Operations Analytics
- Fuel efficiency and optimization insights
- Operational punctuality (OTP) monitoring
- Route and fleet utilization analytics
- Performance benchmarking

### 2. Engineering & Maintenance Analytics
- Reliability and delay attribution analysis
- Component and system performance monitoring
- Maintenance planning support indicators
- Operational disruption intelligence (AOG-aware)

### 3. Network & Commercial Analytics
- Load factor and yield intelligence
- Route performance evaluation
- Capacity planning support metrics
- Revenue-impact visibility

### 4. Customer Journey Analytics
- Disruption impact on passengers
- End-to-end journey visibility
- Service recovery performance indicators
- Experience-driven operational insights

### 5. Executive & Strategic Analytics
- Enterprise KPI framework
- Cross-departmental performance views
- Decision-support dashboards
- Long-term trend and risk visibility

---

## Platform Architecture Overview

SkyMetrics follows a modular analytics architecture:

- **Data Sources**: Operational, Engineering, Commercial, CX systems
- **Data Layer**: Structured, validated, and audit-ready datasets
- **Analytics Layer**: KPI models, metrics, and analytical logic
- **Visualization Layer**: Power BI / Tableau executive dashboards
- **Decision Layer**: Strategy, optimization, and leadership insights

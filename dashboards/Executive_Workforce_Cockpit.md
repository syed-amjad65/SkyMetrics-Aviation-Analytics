# Executive Workforce Cockpit — SkyMetrics™ 2026

## Purpose
This dashboard provides executive leadership with a high-level view of workforce requirements, planning, and risk for Riyadh Air Flight Operations, Engineering, and Ground Services.

It connects **resource planning models** with **operational scenarios** for actionable insights.
